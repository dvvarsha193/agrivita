Feature 05

Government Schemes

Central and state agricultural support programs.

Central

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

₹6,000/year directly to farmer families in 3 equal installments of ₹2,000 each. All landholding farmers are eligible.

Apply at pmkisan.gov.in →
Central

PM Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop insurance at very low premium — 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, 5% for commercial crops. Protects against natural calamities.

Apply at pmfby.gov.in →
Credit

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Short-term credit up to ₹3 lakh at subsidised interest rates of 4–7% per annum. Covers crop and post-harvest expenses.

Apply at your nearest bank →
Central

Soil Health Card Scheme

Free soil testing with NPK and micronutrient analysis. Personalised fertiliser recommendations to improve yield and reduce cost.

Visit nearest KVK →
Central

PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana

Subsidies for drip and sprinkler irrigation — up to 55% for small farmers. Promotes "More Crop Per Drop" efficiency.

Apply via state agriculture dept →
Central

eNAM – National Agri Market

Online trading platform connecting farmers to buyers across India. Get best prices without going to mandi physically.

Register at enam.gov.in →
Infrastructure

Agriculture Infrastructure Fund

₹1 lakh crore fund for post-harvest management, cold chain, and storage infrastructure at concessional rates.

Apply via bank/NBFCs →
Central

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas

Support for organic farming clusters — ₹50,000/hectare over 3 years. Certification and market linkage support included.

Apply via state agriculture dept →

About AgriVita

Built for Indian Farmers, By Indian Students

AgriVita is a B.Tech 2nd Year CSE project. Every farmer deserves access to the same tools that large agribusinesses use. We built this platform to bridge that gap.
