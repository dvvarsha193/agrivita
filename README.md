# 🌱 AgriVita – Smart Agriculture Platform for India
## 📌 Overview
**AgriVita** is a smart agriculture web platform designed to support **farmers** and also help **people with zero knowledge of farming**.
The project focuses on solving one of the biggest problems in agriculture:
> ❗ Lack of awareness and access to real-time, easy-to-understand information.
This platform bridges the gap between **farmers and modern technology** by providing simple, accessible, and multilingual solutions.
---
## 🚜 Problem Statement
Many farmers in India:
* Lack awareness of crop selection, weather updates, and market prices
* Depend on middlemen due to lack of direct information
* Miss government schemes due to low awareness
* Face losses due to poor decision-making
AgriVita solves this by providing a **single digital platform** for all essential farming needs. 
---
## 💡 Features
### 🌾 Core Features
* 📅 Crop Calendar – Know what to grow and when
* 🌱 Smart Crop Suggestion – Based on soil, season & water
* 🌦️ Weather Intelligence – Local weather updates
* 📈 Live Mandi Prices – Real-time market rates
* 🏛️ Government Schemes – Awareness of farmer benefits
* 🤖 AI Agri Chatbot – Ask farming-related questions
### 🤝 Community & Support
* 👨‍🌾 Farmer–Farmer Connect (exchange resources)
* 🧊 Cold Storage Finder (reduce post-harvest loss)
### 🌐 Accessibility Features
* 🌍 Multi-language support (English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam) 
* 📱 Works on low-end devices
* 🧠 Simple UI for low digital literacy users
